2D1N Mount-Kinabalu Climbing
Mount Kinabalu is the tallest mountain in Malaysia. The summit, Low's Peak, sits at 4095.2 meters (13,435 feet) above sea level. It is also the 20th tallest mountain in the world by topographic prominence and is one of the highest peaks in the world that is easily trekked by most people.

Fireflies Watching River-Cruise
The Weston Wetland Park is located about 120 kilometres south of Kota Kinabalu. At sunset, fireflies light up the river creating a magical scene that'll be sure to captivate you and leave you breathless, yearning for more soul-catching moments in life.

This ideal quick getaway from Kota Kinabalu to explore the best spots in Kundasang in a day takes your trip up a notch with some ATV adventure!
