Beauty of Sabah

Kota Kinabalu embraces a fascinating scene of flora and fauna influences. The Mount Kinabalu and beautiful coastal in this delightful capital of Sabah earned it the prestigious title as a UNESCO World Heritage site. To celebrate the distinctive heritage of natural Sabah, Bao Bao Hotel has lovingly restored a series of conservation shophouses and the intricate architectural features to offer our guests the opportunity to experience the unique charms of Kota Kinabalu's nature and illustrious views. Each room is thoughtfully appointed to ensure our guests enjoy modern comforts even as they savor the beauty and historical splendor of Sabah.
